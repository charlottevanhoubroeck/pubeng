BECAUSE SHE'S WORTH IT

Pencil on paper, 297 x 420 mm

In her compilation of short stories 'To the City' ('Naar de stad') (2012), Annelies Verbeke tells a witty but sensible short story about a woman in grief. Her grief seemed so bitter, lovable and hairy, it inspired me to create ‘Because she’s worth it’. A cartoon about slumbering rage, vulnerable pride, and also a bit about sweet-scented shampoo.
